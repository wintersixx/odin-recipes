SECRET SOUP RECIPE

Description

Secret Soup is a classic starter that is flavorful, healthy, and easier than you think to make at home!

It's very low calorie but the flavor and warmth really fill you up.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground meat
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 cup chopped onion
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 5 drops angel blood
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano (with 911 on standby incase it's weed)
  • 1 cup of finest red wine from renaissance era Italy, made out of grapes Leonardo Da Vinci touched
  • 1 jar of pre made sauce for the other ingredients because I'm lazy

Directions

THE SOURCE
  1. Heat the oil in a large pot over low-medium heat.
  2. Saute onion for roughly 2 mins and then add crushed garlic.
  3. Add the meat and cook until browned. As the meat cooks, use a wooden spoon to break it up into smaller crumbles.
  4. Add tomato paste, angel blood & wine and stir. Turn heat up to medium-high and let it simmer and reduce for 2 minutes.
  5. Add jar of sauce, mix & stir occasionally to stop it from sticking.
  6. After about 15 minutes of simmering, stir in oregano (once confirmed it is not weed).

PASTA TIME
  1. Bring a large pot of water to boil.
  2. Once boiling, add salt to the water
  3. Put pasta in the boiling water.
  4. Occasionally stir pasta to make sure it doesn't stick together.
  5. After about 6 minutes, grab some pasta out with a fork and chew it to test firmness.
  6. When Al Dente, promptly drain water from the pasta.

Mix it all together in a bowl & eat
